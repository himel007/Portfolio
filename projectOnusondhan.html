Project Details

This project was basically made for the students of Software Engineering department. The main purpose of this project is to read the rountine from the excel file (format .xlsx) and then generate the routine to the students and teachers. Students can get the routine by entering their semester and section. Teachers can get their routine by entering their initials. Teachers and students can also download their routine in pdf form.

The second purpose is to find bug in the routine. Only admin can access that part. It can find 10 types of bugs of the routine for students and teachers.
